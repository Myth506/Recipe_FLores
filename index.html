Spaghetti Carbonara Recipe

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Pancetta or Bacon
  • 2 Eggs
  • Grated Parmesan Cheese
  • Black Pepper
  • Salt

Cook

  1. Boil a pot of water and cook the spaghetti until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a separate pan, cook the diced pancetta or bacon until crispy.
  3. Beat the eggs in a bowl and mix in grated Parmesan cheese.
  4. Add the cooked spaghetti to the pan with pancetta. Mix well.
  5. Remove from heat and quickly pour the egg and cheese mixture over the pasta. Stir vigorously.
  6. Season with black pepper and a pinch of salt to taste.
  7. Serve hot, garnished with extra Parmesan cheese if desired.
